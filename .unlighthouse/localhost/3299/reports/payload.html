About Me

Yo! My name is Jack and I'm a software engineer based in Auckland, New Zealand. I studied at the University of Auckland and graduated with a Bachelor of Engineering (Honours) in Software Engineering. Out of all aspects of software engineering, I enjoy web development the most. I believe that the design and user experience of a website are just as or if not more important than the functionality in most cases so I've spent a lot of time learning about front-end development.

Outside of sitting in front of my computer, I love to hit the gym, go on hikes and watch the UFC at The Kingslander bar. Another thing I've been engaged in during my free time is freelancing with my friend. Since early 2023, we started offering web design services around our neighborhood. This has been a fun way to apply our skills and make a positive impact in our community. You can find us at Astro Media.
